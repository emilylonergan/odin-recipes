Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delightful Italian recipe that can be prepared for treating someone special. Loaded with cheese, it can be served as a main dish and also be served in small portions as a starter recipe. If you want to surprise your guests with some innovative dish, try out this easy recipe!.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilograms ground mutton
  • 2 clove minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 8 ounce ricotta cheese
  • 9 sliced & slit lasagne sheets
  • 3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 180 ml tomato puree
  • 2 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 pinches oregano

Steps

  1. Cook the pork and mutton meat
  2. Prepare the meat sauce
  3. Mix all the cheese with eggs
  4. Make the layering using cheese, lasagne sheet and meat sauce
  5. Bake the lasagna for 50 minutes at 375 degrees Fahrenheit
